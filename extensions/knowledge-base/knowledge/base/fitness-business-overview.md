# Online Fitness Coaching — Business Overview

## How the Business Works

Online fitness coaches sell:

- 1:1 coaching programs (typically 8–16 weeks)
- Group programs or challenges
- Digital products (plans, guides)
- Memberships and communities

## Key Operations

- **Lead Generation:** ads, social media, referrals → leads enter CRM
- **Sales Process:** lead → DM/call → offer presented → closed or followed up
- **Onboarding:** payment taken → intake form → program delivered → welcome sequence
- **Delivery:** check-ins, Kahunas workouts, nutrition tracking, support via Slack/Telegram/DM
- **Retention & Upsell:** monthly reviews, re-sign offers, referral requests
- **Reporting:** weekly KPI reviews (leads, calls booked, closes, revenue, churn)

## Common Team Roles

- Head Coach / Owner
- Sales Closer
- Client Success / Support
- Operations / Admin VA

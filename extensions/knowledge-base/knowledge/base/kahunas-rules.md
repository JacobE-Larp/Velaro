# Kahunas — Working Rules

## What Kahunas Is Used For

- Delivering fitness and nutrition programs to clients
- Tracking client progress (workouts, check-ins, measurements)
- Messaging clients inside the platform

## Key Rules

- Assign programs by matching the correct plan to the client's goal and level
- Always confirm the client is active (not cancelled/paused) before assigning new content
- Log check-in outcomes in the client's notes for the coach to review
- When a client is onboarded, confirm their program is assigned and first check-in is scheduled

## Common Tasks

- Assign a workout program on signup
- Log weekly check-in results
- Flag clients who have missed check-ins for 7+ days
- Update nutrition targets when coach requests

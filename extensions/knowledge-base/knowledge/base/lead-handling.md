# Lead Handling Rules

## Lead Journey

1. Lead enters CRM (from ad, DM, referral, form)
2. Initial contact within 15 minutes where possible
3. Qualify: goal, budget, timeline, previous coaching
4. Book a call or move to async close
5. Follow up until closed or disqualified

## Rules

- Always tag a lead with their source (e.g. "fb-ad", "instagram-dm", "referral")
- Move to the correct pipeline stage after every interaction
- If no response after 3 follow-ups, tag "cold" and move to nurture sequence
- Never mark a lead as "lost" without logging the reason
- If a lead books, confirm the booking and send a reminder 24 hours before

## CRM Notes Format

Date: [YYYY-MM-DD]
Action: [what was done]
Outcome: [result]
Next Step: [what happens next]
